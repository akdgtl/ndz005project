Getting Your Loan

  • Phone call request & a finance specialist will guide you through the whole application process.

  • You are required to submit some documents for approval process.

  • The maximum amount you can borrow is R4500.00 and if approved, the loaned money will be paid electronically within 24 to 48 hours.

  • Offer from 1-3 months term loans.

Payment Schedule

  • Terms are flexible & discussed to suit your capability.

  • Cash Loans must be repaid within 3months

  • Repayment is automatically deducted from the bank account you provided, it should have enough balance to pay the loan installment when the agreed repayment schedule comes

  • Failure to pay your balance on time can result in fees & penalties

Requirements

  • SA citizen, age 18+

  • ID book

  • 3 months bank statement

  • Proof of residence

  • Most recent payslips
